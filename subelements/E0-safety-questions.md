## Group E0A — Safety: RF radiation hazards; exposure limits; hazardous materials; station safety

### E0A01
**What is the primary function of an external earth connection or ground rod?**
- A) Prevent static build up on power lines
- **B) Lightning charge dissipation** ✅
- C) Reduce RF current flow between pieces of equipment
- D) Protect breaker panel from power surges

> The correct answer is Lightning charge dissipation.

### E0A02
**When evaluating RF exposure levels from your station at a neighbor’s home, what must you do?**
- A) Ensure signals from your station are less than the controlled maximum permissible exposure (MPE)
- **B) Ensure signals from your station are less than the uncontrolled maximum permissible exposure (MPE)** ✅
- C) Ensure signals from your station are less than the controlled maximum permissible emission (MPE)
- D) Ensure signals from your station are less than the uncontrolled maximum permissible emission (MPE)

> The correct answer is Ensure signals from your station are less than the uncontrolled maximum permissible exposure (MPE).

### E0A03
**Over what range of frequencies are the FCC human body RF exposure limits most restrictive?**
- A) 300 kHz - 3 MHz
- B) 3 - 30 MHz
- **C) 30 - 300 MHz** ✅
- D) 300 - 3000 MHz

> The correct answer is C) 30 - 300 MHz.

### E0A04
**When evaluating a site with multiple transmitters operating at the same time, the operators and licensees of which transmitters are responsible for mitigating over-exposure situations?**
- A) Each transmitter that produces 20 percent or more of its MPE limit in areas where the total MPE limit
- B) Each transmitter operating with a duty cycle greater than 25 percent
- **C) Each transmitter that produces 5 percent or more of its MPE limit in areas where the total MPE limit** ✅
- D) Each transmitter operating with a duty cycle greater than 50 percent

> The correct answer is Each transmitter that produces 5 percent or more of its MPE limit in areas where the total MPE limit.

### E0A05
**What hazard is created by operating at microwave frequencies?**
- A) Microwaves are ionizing radiation
- **B) The high gain antennas commonly used can result in high exposure levels** ✅
- C) Microwaves are in the frequency range where wave velocity is higher
- D) The extremely high frequency energy can damage the joints of antenna structures

> The correct answer is B) The high gain antennas commonly used can result in high exposure levels.

### E0A06
**Why are there separate electric (E) and magnetic (H) MPE limits at frequencies below 300 MHz?**
- A) The body reacts to electromagnetic radiation from both the E and H fields
- B) Ground reflections and scattering cause the field strength to vary with location
- C) E field and H field radiation intensity peaks can occur at different locations
- **D) All these choices are correct** ✅

### E0A07
**What is meant by “100% tie-off” regarding tower safety?**

- C) All tools secured to the climber’s harness

### E0A08
**What does SAR measure?**

> The rate at which RF energy is absorbed by the body is the correct term or definition for this question.

### E0A09

### E0A10
